## APPENDIX: METHODOLOGY

**EAN Matching**: Normalized scientific notation to 13-digit strings, exact equality required
**Title Matching**: Token-based similarity with brand/pack size consistency checks  
**Profitability**: UK VAT-registered calculations including Input/Output VAT on fees
**Risk Assessment**: Based on product category logic, margin realism, and match confidence
**Sales Data**: Extracted from "bought_in_past_month" and Keepa availability indicators
