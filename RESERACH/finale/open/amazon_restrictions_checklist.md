# Amazon UK FBA Restriction & Hazmat Conservative Checklist

## 1. Dangerous Goods (Hazmat) - STOP & VERIFY
If any of these keywords appear in Title, Description, or Bullets, assume HAZMAT until proven otherwise via Seller Central 'Add a Product' (Search ASIN > Show limitations).

### Keywords (Case Insensitive):
- **Batteries**: `battery`, `lithium`, `ion`, `power bank`, `charged`, `electric` (if containing battery).
- **Flammables**: `aerosol`, `spray`, `gas`, `lighter`, `fuel`, `butane`, `compressed air`.
- **Chemicals**: `cleaner`, `bleach`, `acid`, `corrosive`, `solvent`, `paint`, `ink`, `toner`, `glue`, `adhesive`.
- **Fragrances/Alcohol**: `perfume`, `cologne`, `essential oil`, `diffuser`, `alcohol`, `sanitizer`, `mouthwash` (some).
- **Other**: `magnet` (strong ones), `pest control` (poisons).

### Visible Signals on Amazon PDP:
- "This item is non-returnable" (Common for hazmat/grocery/custom, but strong hazmat indicator if non-grocery).
- "Expedited shipping not available" (Often hazmat).
- "Batteries Required: Yes".

## 2. Gating & Brand Restrictions - RISK ASSESSMENT
Brands and categories often requiring specific approval (Invoice gating).

### High Risk Categories:
- **Topicals**: Creams, lotions, shampoos, soaps (Need invoices, strict safety).
- **Grocery**: Food/Drink (Expiration dates, meltable).
- **Baby**: Feeding, safety gear.
- **Toys**: Educational, branded (LEGO, Disney).
- **Pet**: Food, treats, topical treatments.

### Brand Gating Indicators:
- **Major Brands**: Adidas, Nike, LEGO, Apple, Samsung, Sony, Bosch, etc. usually require un-gating.
- **Listing Discrepancy**: Brand on Amazon is "Generic" but product is branded -> IP Complaint Risk (Counterfeit).
- **Exclusive**: "Exclusive to Amazon" or sold only by Brand Owner.

## 3. FBA Eligibility & Prep
Physical attributes that affect FBA ability or fees.

### Checks:
- **Meltable**: Chocolate, gummies, wax (Apr 15 - Oct 15 restriction).
- **Glass/Fragile**: Requires bubble wrap/boxing (Add prep fee).
- **Liquids**: Double seal required (Add prep fee if not sealed).
- **Sharp**: Knives, scissors (blister pack required).
- **Hazmat FBA**: Even if hazmat approved, FBA Hazmat program capacity is limited.

## 4. Verification Workflow (Row-by-Row)
1. **Keyword Scan**: Check Title for Hazmat keywords.
2. **Page Check**:
   - Verify EAN match (Ctrl+F on page if visible, or via Keepa/Extensions).
   - Check "Return Policy".
   - Check "Batteries Required".
   - Check Seller Count (If 1 seller = Brand Owner? If 0 FBA sellers = Hazmat?).
3. **Decision**:
   - **PASS**: No keywords, Returns accepted, FBA sellers present, EAN matches.
   - **HOLD**: Hazmat keywords present, "Generic" brand mismatch, Login required for supplier.
   - **AVOID**: Clearly Hazmat (Aerosol), IP Risk (Counterfeit), Negative Profit.
