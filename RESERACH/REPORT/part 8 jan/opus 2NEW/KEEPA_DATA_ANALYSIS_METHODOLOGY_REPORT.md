## 📈 KEEPA DATA INTERPRETATION GUIDE

### Understanding the Sales Rank Graph

| BSR Position | Interpretation | Monthly Sales Est. |
|--------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Under 5,000 | 🔥 Elite - Top 0.01% | 1,000+ units |
| 5,000 - 25,000 | ✅ Excellent - High velocity | 300-1,000 units |
| 25,000 - 50,000 | ✅ Good - Consistent sales | 100-300 units |
| 50,000 - 100,000 | ⚠️ Moderate - Slower moving | 50-100 units |
| 100,000 - 250,000 | ⚠️ Slow - Niche product | 20-50 units |
| Over 250,000 | ❌ Very Slow - Avoid | <20 units |

### Understanding Price Line Colors (Keepa Chart)

| Line Color | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| **Blue** | Amazon direct price (when Amazon sells) |
| **Orange/Yellow** | Buy Box price (winning offer) |
| **Red/Pink** | New 3rd party lowest price |
| **Black** | Used items |
| **Green** (Sales Rank) | BSR trend line |

### Red Flags in Keepa Data

| Pattern | What It Means | Action |
|---------|---------------|--------|
| Gaps in Buy Box line | Suppressed Buy Box | ❌ Avoid |
| BSR spikes upward suddenly | Sales dropped sharply | ⚠️ Investigate |
| Price drops below 50% | Potential liquidation | ⚠️ Check reviews |
| Amazon blue line appears | Amazon now competing | ⚠️ Margin pressure |
| Single thin price line | Only 1 seller | ⚠️ Check if gated |
