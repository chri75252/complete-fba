"## Executive Summary"  
""  
"This memo synthesizes authoritative external guidance on structuring AI-assisted wholesale supplier discovery workflows. Key findings support: (a) targeted, respectful crawling over full-site extraction; (b) multi-layer supplier verification using government registries, brand authorization checks, and invoice compliance validation; (c) structured product intelligence logging with unique identifier matching (EAN/UPC/ASIN); and (d) browser-assisted validation using price history tools like Keepa."
