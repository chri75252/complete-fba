- `Adjusted Profit` only changes for clear integer multipack mismatches (e.g., Amazon title says 3× but supplier looks single-unit).
- Amazon pages can intermittently require captcha; `amazon_title_unknown` means the fetch was blocked or lacked a stable title element.
