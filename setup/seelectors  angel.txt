# Angel Wholesale (angelwholesale.co.uk) - CSS Selectors

## PRODUCT LISTING PAGE SELECTORS

### Product Item Container
```css
#product-listing-container article
```
*Note: Each product is contained within an article element under the product-listing-container*

### Title (on listing page)
```css
article h3 a
```
*Alternative: The product title appears in heading tags within each article*

### Price (on listing page)
```css
article .price--withoutTax
```
*The main price displays without tax information*

### Product URL (on listing page)
```css
article h3 a[href]
```
*Or: `article a[href*="/p"]` for product links*

### Out of Stock Indicator
```
article .out-of-stock-message
```
*Note: Products display "Out of stock" text when unavailable. In listing: text appears near product*

***

## INDIVIDUAL PRODUCT PAGE SELECTORS

### Product Title
```css
.productView h1.productView-title
```
*Alternative: `h1.productView-title` or main heading on product page*

### Price
```css
.productView-price .price--withoutTax
```
*Main product price without VAT*

### Price Login Required
```
.productView .price-login-required
```
*Note: This site displays prices publicly. If login required, text would appear instead of price*

### EAN/Barcode
```
Product Specification section: Look for "Barcode" label
CSS: `.productView-info dt:contains('Barcode') + dd`
```
*Barcode appears in the PRODUCT SPECIFICATION table with label "Barcode"*
*Example values found: "5012128621741", "5012128616792"*

### Stock Status / Availability
```css
.productView-info .availability
```
*Text format: "AVAILABILITY: 48 IN STOCK" or "AVAILABILITY: 96 IN STOCK"*

### SKU
```css
.productView-info .sku
```
*Format: "SKU: C19593" - appears next to availability*

### Out of Stock (product page)
```
.productView .out-of-stock-badge
```
*Would display when product is unavailable*

***

## CATEGORY/SEARCH RESULT PAGE SELECTORS

### Next Page Button
```css
a:contains('LOAD MORE')
```
*This site uses infinite scroll with a "LOAD MORE" link button*
*No traditional pagination - products load dynamically*

### Price (in results)
```css
article .price--withoutTax
```
*Same as product listing price selector*

### Product Title (in results)
```css
article h3 a
```
*Same as product listing title selector*

### Product URL (in results)
```css
article h3 a[href]
```
*Same as product listing URL selector*

## ADDITIONAL NOTES

1. **Product Container Structure**: Products on listing pages are contained in `<article>` elements within `#product-listing-container`

2. **Bulk Pricing**: Products show tiered pricing like "Buy 24+ for £0.81 each" - these appear as separate elements

3. **Sale Badge**: Sale items have a badge with class containing "SALE"

4. **Assorted Designs**: Some products show "Assorted Designs" indicator with an info icon

5. **Images**: Product images use class `.card-image` or similar within article containers

6. **Price Format**: Prices shown are exclusive of VAT, format: "£0.86 each"

7. **Stock Status Format**: 
   - In Stock: "AVAILABILITY: X IN STOCK" (where X is quantity)
   - Out of Stock: Would show "Out of stock" badge/text

8. **Pagination**: Uses "LOAD MORE" button for infinite scroll rather than traditional numbered pagination
